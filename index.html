Step Guidelines
How many steps per day for weight loss?
🚶 Baseline health 7,000–8,000
⚡ Active weight loss 10,000–12,000
🔥 Accelerated loss 12,000–15,000
Tip: Every 2,000 steps burns roughly 80–100 Cal depending on your weight. Breaking it up — morning walk + evening walk — is just as effective as one long session.
